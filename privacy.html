Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Paw Clock is a pet feeding tracker made by Spring Data Limited. This policy explains what information the app uses, where that information is stored, and how you can contact us with privacy questions.

Short version: Paw Clock stores your pet data locally on your device so the app and widget can work. We do not run ads, we do not sell your data, and we do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Information Paw Clock stores

The app may store the information you enter, including:

  • Pet names, species, food profiles, meal schedules, dietary notes, and reminder settings
  • Household and caregiver names entered in the app
  • Feeding history, including timestamps and the caregiver name attached to each log entry
  • Medication and supplement routines if you add them
  • Subscription entitlement state needed to unlock Pro features on your device

Where data is stored

Paw Clock currently stores app data locally on your device. The app also writes a compact widget snapshot into the app group container so Home Screen widgets can display the latest pet status. We do not store this data on our own servers.

Camera, photo library, and food label scans

If you choose to scan a food label, Paw Clock can use the camera or a photo you select from your photo library. The app uses on-device text recognition to suggest food details for you to review before saving. We do not upload label photos to Spring Data Limited servers.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Paw Clock schedules local reminders for upcoming or overdue meals and care routines. Notification settings stay on your device unless Apple requires limited processing to deliver the local notification you requested.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card information. Apple may provide us with transaction status and receipt information required to unlock purchased features on your device.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell pet or household data.
  • We do not use third-party ad networks.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not upload your food label photos to our own servers.

Your choices

  • You can edit or delete pets, feeding logs, caregivers, and care routines inside the app.
  • You can turn notifications off in the app or in iOS Settings.
  • You can choose not to use food label scanning.

Children

Paw Clock is a general pet care utility and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own backend services because the app does not operate such a collection service at this time.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact hello@springdata.uk.
